hi I'm Vincent!

After years of working in industrial design, I felt my true passion and strength is user research and UX design. While I enjoy designing beautiful products, I want to go beyond sheer styling. I want my next job to be design thinking oriented. I therefore moved to San Francisco in 2018 to pursue a new career in UX design. I'm now half way through the MFA program at the Academy of Art University and I'm looking for opportunities. If you need a UX design intern or junior UX/product designer, please contact me.

Many skills I acquired from industrial design are transferable to software development. Some of the transferable skills include working and communicating with engineers and business units, design thinking, sketching, and styling sense. Hardware design also involves a fair amount of UX and problem solving. I’ve been a human centered design practitioner for many years prior to grad school. Lastly, I’ve shipped many hardware products and know how to design for production. In order to design products that can be mass produced and sell well, I had to design under technical limitations, financial constraints, and market demands. All of these are applicable to software development.
