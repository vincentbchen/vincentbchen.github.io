brand mantra

Nuc is a young watch brand that was founded in 2015 by a former Swatch designer and a former Adidas designer. Our target audience are youth between the age of 15-25 who likes casual sporty fashion that is minimal and colorful, just like ourselves. Nuc is the last three alphabet of gerenuk, a type of African antelope. We picked this name because it’s short, memorable, easy to pronounce, and sounds fashionable & sporty at the same time. Our 4 brand pillars are young, sporty fashion, playful, and colorful.
