AR GAME
Fantasy Cyber Warrior is an Augmented Reality first person shooter game aiming to appeal to female gamers

problem definition

Only 7% of FPS gamers are female. The only exception is Overwatch. Overwatch has 15.2% female players, more than twice the average in this category. The aim of this project is to determine why women don’t like to play FPS games and then design an AR FPS game that can appeal to female gamers.

project goals

  1. Research and find out why female gamers don’t like to play first person shooter games
  2. Design a first person shooter game that is more appealing to female gamers without losing male gamers

He has designed many successful games for different demographics, including female gamers. I also interviewed 6 gamers between the ages of 19-27. secondary research

The diagram below shows that male and female gamers have different gaming motivations. The top two male gaming motivations are competition and destruction. The top two female gaming motivations are completion and fantasy. Sci-Fi and fantasy are two genres both male and female gamers like . I decided to design a FPS game that’s a hybrid of science fiction and fantasy.

Why don’t girls like to play FPS games?

In game harassment

There are lots of trash talk and catcalls during games. Girls constantly get stereotyped and sexually harassed.

Social

Not enough of their female friends want to play FPS games. Social interaction is one of the most important aspects of gaming.

Parenting & Childhood Experience

Some parents won’t let their daughters play violent games. Most girls don’t have sweet childhood memories of FPS games.

Don’t like guns, Destruction, & Violence

Some women don’t like guns, destruction, gore, and violence. Male and female gamers have different motivations

Game doesn’t offer different types of Roles

Many women don’t want to fight in frontlines. They prefer to play supporting roles such as healing.

Lack of customization/in Game Cosmetics

Most FPS games don’t have enough customization/in game cosmetics for their characters.

lack of narrative

Most FPS games don’t have stories behind characters, items, or environments. Female players care a lot about story and content.

Not attracted to the design

Most FPS don’t have designs and art styles girls are drawn to.

PERSONA

This game has 3 target persona: light-core social gamer, mid-core gaming enthusiast, and hardcore gaming addict. The primary difference is the number of hours they play per week. It varies from a few hours per week to 12+ hours per day. While female gamers get harassed a lot more frequently, male players also receive a fair amount of trash talks, insults, and unpleasant comments from toxic players during games. The common thread that ties them together is their love for science fiction, fantasy, and the social aspect of gaming.

Harassment Report

Gamers can file harassment reports via the cellphone app. The app has a complete history of gameplays with exact date, time, and players in each game.

Player Profile with Ethics Ranking

Toxic players have ways to ramp up their profile reviews and ratings, so I decided to use a rating system that's similar to Michelin stars and eBay.

Create a social gaming experience

Gamers sign-up with social media profiles, so the app can match players with friends. AR games are on-site, which allows them to make friends while playing games.

Alternative Weapons

Have alternative weapons such as magic spells, magic arrows, and cards in addition to guns and explosives.

Alternative Roles

Have different types of roles such as healers, tanks and the like. Players who prefer to play supporting roles can be healers.

Female Friendly Themes and Designs

Sci-fi and fantasy games appeal to both men and women. I therefore design a fantasy and science fiction fusion FPS game.

social pairing

People want to hang out with others of similar age, interest, and personality. Pairing player with similar attributes can promote social interactions and reduce conflicts

Competency ranking and pairing

Much of in-game harassments is noob bashing. Pairing players with similar competencies and game difficulties and avoid noob bashing

AR GAME WALK THROUGH

character selection

The game starts with character selection. There are 3 types of characters/weapons for players to choose from: archer, magician, and shooter. Since many female gamers don’t like guns, there are other choices of weapons for them. Players who like guns have several guns to choose from as well.

*The AR goggles and weapons are not original. Please see the citation section for details.

ARCHER

Archers attack with arrows. There are 4 types of arrows: frost arrow, flame arrow, magic arrow, and physical arrow. Frost arrow will freeze the enemy units for 3-6 seconds and slow down their movement speed. Flame arrow will lit the enemy units on fire. Magic arrows will trigger a magic explosion upon hitting the enemy units. Physical arrow will damage the enemy units but does not have any special effects. Some arrows can deal more damages than others, depending on which types of enemy units. Players have to select the arrow carefully according to the type of enemy units they are attacking.

*The AR goggles and weapons are not original. Please see the citation section for details.

magician

Magicians attack with their hands and have 4 different abilities, small fireball, big fire ball, healing, and magic shields. Players can throw small fireballs to deal quick and small damages. When they charge the fireball, it’ll continue to grow in size and deals more damage. Magicians are also healers and can heal nearby ally units. This ability has cool down. Magicians can also cast magic shields to block or reflect magic attacks from enemy units. Magicians can only produce magic damage, which are not as effective towards physical units. Players can also choose to be a healer and support the team.

*The AR goggles and magic symbols are not original. Please see the citation section for details.

shooter

Shooters attack with guns. Players can choose between a pair of small handguns or a large laser gun. Shooter can also cast a force shield to block physical attacks from enemy units. Shooters can only deal physical damage, which is not as effective towards magic units. Shooter can also be the tank for the team if the player prefers to play a supporting role.

*The AR goggles and weapons are not original. Please see the citation section for details.

interface overview

1. Remaining health point of a teammate
2. Your remaining health point
3. Power bar: Some abilities can be charged up to 10 seconds to increase the damage of the attack

4. Remaining time
5. Crosshair: It shows the destination of your attack
6. Radar: it shows the locations of ally and enemy units within 10 meter radius

game play basic

Players can toggle between different abilities by pressing the buttons on their forearm. Viewport will blink and turn blue for a second when the player gets hit. The viewport will turn red when they player’s health point is below 10%. The player will also hear a heartbeat sound. The viewport will turn black when the player dies. Respawn time will be displayed in the center of the screen.
