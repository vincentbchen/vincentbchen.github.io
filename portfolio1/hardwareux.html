samsung cooling india

Samsung Cooling India is an air conditioner user experience project I did for a strategic consultancy (cannot disclose their name due to their request). Many great western products failed in India because they are not suitable for the Indian cultures and lifestyles. Samsung came to this consultancy and asked them to research and design a few air conditioner UX concepts that are suitable for the Indian market. This project is comprised of 3 phases: research, concept development, and concept testing. I only participated in the concept development phase. I was tasked to analyze research findings and translate them into air conditioner UX concepts. I was part of a team of 4 people: a studio partner, a senior project manager, and a senior interaction designer. Process deck available upon request.

pill time

Pill Time is a pill management system for elder chronic patients who have to take lots of pills (10-20) several times everyday. More than 120,000 Americans die each year due to prescription medication non-compliance, twice the number killed in automotive accidents. I decided to design a system of products that can help them to take their medication on time and stay healthy. The goals of this project are:


  1. Discover problems elderly patients are facing with taking medications
  2. Discover problems with current pill management products and design opportunities
  3. Design a pill management system that can solve these problems

I did a qualitative research to interview 6 elder chronic patients and an in-house nurse who takes care of 2 of them. I also did extensive secondary research and competitive analysis. In addition, I purchase several pill management products and tried them to find out problems with current products and design opportunities. Process deck available upon request.
